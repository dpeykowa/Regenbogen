RS Spyder V12 873hp.

Our dream was to create a one-of-a-kind open-top car. We made it come true with the RS Spyder V12, an unrivaled gem of a supercar. Equipped with an exclusive technology package, the jewel in this car's crown is its carbon fiber monocoque. The elegance and vanity of a Spyder meet the power of a naturally aspirated 6.5 liter V-12 engine putting out 515 kW (700 CV), with incredible torque available at all engine speeds. The RS Spyder is an open-top supercar that combines sportiness and style.

New Matrix LED technology

We open the new factory of company

The new facility will be built by SUV MOTORS, a joint venture involving Toyota Tsusho and Malaysian conglomerate UMW Holdings, at an estimated cost of 30 billion yen ($275 million). It is slated to come online as early as 2018, churning out 50,000 vehicles a year. The new facility will produce small cars, with annual capacity potentially rising to 100,000 vehicles.
